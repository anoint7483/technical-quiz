a. Which process is used to separate components of a liquid mixture based on the differences in their boiling points?

A. Distillation
B. Filtration
C. Evaporation
D. Crystallization
Answer: A. Distillation

b. Who was the first human to journey into outer space?

A. Yuri Gagarin
B. Neil Armstrong
C. Buzz Aldrin
D. Valentina Tereshkova
Answer: A. Yuri Gagarin
